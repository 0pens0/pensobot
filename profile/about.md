# About

I'm **Oren Penso**, a technical leader who sits at the intersection of **platform engineering, cloud-native application delivery, and secure AI adoption**.

I spend most of my time helping teams answer one question:

> "How do we ship software faster *without* losing control over security, reliability, and cost?"

## What I do

Professionally, I operate in a **Field CTO / C-level strategy advisory** lane: I serve as a trusted technical advisor to C-level executives and help organisations translate **business goals into technical requirements** (and back)—using platform strategy, reference architectures, and the right technology tools so strategy becomes execution.

I advise, design, and prototype systems that turn platform strategy into real developer outcomes—especially in complex enterprise environments.

My "happy place" is where:
- platforms meet real org constraints (risk, regulation, audit, cost)
- cloud-native meets legacy realities
- AI ambitions meet data and security boundaries

## What I believe

- **Platforms are products**: they need UX, documentation, feedback loops, and clear ownership.
- **Guardrails beat gates**: enable teams by default; block only when necessary.
- **Security is a design property**, not a checklist at the end.
- **Observability is non-negotiable**: if you can't see it, you can't operate it.
- **Great architecture is pragmatic**: the best solution is the one teams can run on Monday.

I value:
